Valorant

Made by: Jerome Brent A. Tegrado

Introduction

Jump into the Action

I started playing Valorant during the pandemic, which coincided with the time we were provided with a PC for online classes. Initially, I wasn't very skilled at Valorant, so, like many others, I began playing more frequently and dedicated time to training. I especially enjoyed playing with my friends. I still feel nostalgic whenever I play this game because I remember the fun memories of playing with my friends from morning until midnight. Even though we couldn't go outside due to the pandemic, we were able to connect through the game. Valorant helped me overcome feelings of loneliness and brought me friends I could play with.

Our Opinion

Strategic Teamwork

Playing Valorant has its fair share of advantages and challenges. On the positive side, it provided me with enjoyable moments spent with friends and even expanded my social circle as I met new people through the game. It also enhanced my strategic thinking and teamwork skills, as every match required careful planning and coordination. The game’s regular updates and fresh content kept the experience engaging and exciting. However, there are also downsides. I noticed that I became more prone to frustration, especially when my performance was poor or when my teammates didn’t play well. The competitive nature of Valorant sometimes made it challenging to stay calm. Additionally, a stable internet connection is essential to fully enjoy the game, which can be a limitation in certain situations. Despite these drawbacks, the overall experience has been rewarding and memorable.

Conclusion

Celebrating Wins

Valorant has been more than just a game for me during the pandemic. It provided a much-needed escape from loneliness and offered a platform to connect and bond with friends. While it has its challenges, such as moments of frustration, the overall experience has been positive, fostering both personal growth and meaningful relationships. Valorant stands out as a game that not only entertains but also brings people together, making it a valuable addition to my gaming repertoire.

Release Date

June 2, 2020

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. The game combines precise gunplay with unique character abilities, offering a strategic and competitive experience.

FAQ

What platforms is Valorant available on?

Valorant is currently available exclusively on Windows PC, with potential future expansions to other platforms.

Is Valorant free to play?

Yes, Valorant is free to play and can be downloaded from Riot Games’ official website, with optional in-game purchases for cosmetics and battle passes.

What are the system requirements for Valorant?

Valorant runs on most PCs, requiring at least Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit), 4GB RAM, an Intel Core 2 Duo E8400 processor, and Intel HD 3000 graphics.
